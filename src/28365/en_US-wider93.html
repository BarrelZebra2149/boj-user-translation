점화식과 주기

문제

Given a prime number $p$, a recurrence relation $x_n = ax_{n-1} + bx_{n-2} (n \ge 2)$, and initial terms $x_0$, $x_1$, the existence of integers $S \ge 0, T > 0$ which satisfy the following condition is guaranteed by the pigeonhole principle.

Condition: For every integer $n$ greater than or equal to $S$, $x_{n+T} - x_{n}$is a multiple $p$

The problem is to find the smallest of $(S,T)$ in alphabetical order that satisfies the above condition.

입력

In the first line, the integers $p, a, b, x_0, x_1$ are given. $p$ is guaranteed to be prime.

출력

In the first line, output the smallest in alphabetical order among the $(S, T)$ that satisfy the condition.

제한

  • $0 < p \le 2147483647$
  • $0 \le a, b, x_0, x_1 < p$
